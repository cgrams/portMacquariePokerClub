Clock Aware April 5 /2019 Thin but real value of clock watching

I've noticed that with a bit of awarness you can save some good chips at the end of the game by being aware of where the clock is and button. So it might be a good idea to stay in a hand just to delay the game so that the big blind lands on you just before the blinds are raised.

It would be obvious if you do it the hand just before. But you can play a bit creative a round before. You are sheilded from criticism if you are in the hand. So a King 5 offsuit might be worth a look at the flop to control the flow of the game.

Did not cash. All in on Ace Jack off for $25 with $4 in the blind. Two folded and a third call with pocket 2s. Good equity and a bit of a crazy call with 2s but not too crazy because he had chips and could knock out a player.
